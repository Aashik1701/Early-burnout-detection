Problem Statement 1:
Early Detection of Student Burnout & Dropout Risk
Background
Universities often identify academic issues only after performance
drops. By that time, intervention becomes difficult. Early behavioural
signals such as reduced LMS activity, delayed submissions, and
attendance changes can indicate burnout risk.
Challenge
Build a behavioural analytics system that predicts:
• Burnout Risk Level (Low / Medium / High)
• Academic Disengagement Indicators
• Dropout Probability
Possible Data Inputs
Teams may use or simulate:
• LMS login frequency
• Assignment submission delays
• Attendance trends
• Sentiment analysis from feedback forms
• Activity pattern irregularities
Expected Output
Your solution must provide:
• A Risk Score (0–100 or categorical classification)
• Key behavioural triggers influencing the prediction
• Recommended intervention strategy
• Visualization dashboard (optional but encouraged)
