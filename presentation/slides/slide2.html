The Challenge

Student dropout is a global crisis of lost potential.

20–50%

Global dropout rates in higher education institutions.

Double Impact

Every dropout represents lost institutional investment and lost individual student potential.

Why Current Detection Fails

Early Signs (Missed)

Behavioral changes, stress spikes, minor engagement dips go unnoticed.

The Reactive Point

Institutions notice only after grades collapse or attendance is critically low.

Intervention Attempt (Too Late)

Standard outreach sent, but student has often already mentally disengaged.

Core Question

"How can we identify students likely to burn out or drop out early enough — using behavioural and academic signals — so that targeted interventions can be deployed before the point of no return?"

What's Missing Today

No Early Warning

Relies on lagging indicators like grades.

No Personalisation

Generic "at-risk" lists without specific actions.

Resource Blindness

Ignores counselor capacity limits.

Black Box Models

No transparency on *why* a student is flagged.
