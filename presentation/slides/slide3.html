Problem Statement

System Requirements

To effectively solve early dropout, the system must deliver on these six core capabilities.

Early Detection

Flag students using real-time behavioural signals throughout the semester, not just waiting for end-of-term grades.

Actionable Output

Every flagged student receives a personalized intervention recommendation tailored to their specific risk profile.

Dual Operating Modes

Switch between 'Balanced' mode for auditing/reporting and 'High-Recall' mode for proactive student welfare deployment.

Transparency

Per-student behavioral triggers explain exactly *why* the model flagged them, removing the "black box" problem.

Scalability

Batch score 10,000+ students instantly and visualize results in a dashboard designed for non-technical staff.

Resource Awareness

Plan outreach based on actual counselor capacity (weekly slots), prioritizing the most urgent interventions.
